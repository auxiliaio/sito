Over the years I’ve specialised in producing simple yet meaningful solutions to a variety of digital tasks, by creating engaging digital experiences & strategies that better connect companies with their audience.

What do I do?

Website Design & Development, Branding & Visual Identity, Graphic Design & IT Consultancy.

What don't I do?

Run in public. Fall gracefully. Understand the end of Inception. Stretch before exercising. Whittle. Skip queues. Run with scissors. Know how to whistle with two fingers. Stretch after exercising.

What do I want?

If you need results by yesterday, I'm not the best match for you. You’re not investing in the present, you’re investing in your future and so am I (both yours and mine).

Imagining, solving, and delivering solutions take time. In that time, your team and I work together as the same team. That team also includes your clients. We’re like one big happy family.

Expect to learn something new. I love to be challenged and I’ll certainly challenge you. And at the end of the day, when it’s all out on the table, we’ll all learn from each other.

How do I work?

I don’t have any icons or diagrams that explain my creative process. Nor do I have clever names for the stages of each project.

But I do have curiosity.

And I believe that to solve a problem, you need to talk about it. You need to look at it from every angle. To discuss and debate it. To pull it apart, and then put it back together again. And I believe in doing all of these things together.

Conversation, consideration and collaboration. That’s my process.

When I'm not designing, I write about personal tech and listen to podcasts & audiobooks. I'm a minimalist, I'm always in black, I'm a soundtrack buff, a nonconformist, and an old soul.
